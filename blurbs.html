Here are some blurbs that might help out someone someday. They are essentially small writings, mostly mathematical, that serve to exemplify a topic. They are not meant to be comprehensive, but rather to give a taste.



Radical Isogenies on (Twisted) Edwards Curves

In this blurb, we try to find radical isogenies on twisted Edwards curves. We will do so by first looking at what radical isogenies are, and then look at how radical isogenies should work on twisted Edwards curves. Finally, by firing up a computer algebra system, we find the concrete radical isogeny formula for degree 3 on twisted Edwards curves. blurb
